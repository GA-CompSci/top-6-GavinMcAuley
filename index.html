Criteria

Winning Impact

This measures how much a player helps their team win. It includes team record, on/off impact, and overall influence on the game. The best players raise their team’s level.

Two-Way Impact

Great players affect both offense and defense. They score and create for others while also defending well, whether that’s on-ball defense or helping protect the rim.

Individual Production

This is about how much a player produces statistically. It includes points, assists, rebounds, and advanced stats. The best players consistently put up elite numbers and carry a big load for their team.

Playoff Performance

The playoffs matter most. The best players perform at the same level — or better — when the pressure is highest and the competition is strongest.
